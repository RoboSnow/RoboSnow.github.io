Welcome to the 雪他　ロボット(Yukita Robot)

We are a PLTW EDD group from Shakopee High School. Between the three of us we have 8+ years of engineering classes; we are using this combined knowledge to create a robotic snow plow for our cap stone project.

Project

Our project 雪他　ロボット(Yukita Robot), or 雪他 for short, is a robotic snow plow controlled by Arduino. The end goal is to have a working robot that clears a standard two car driveway, and is safer than using a snow blower. The dream goal would be to have a marketable product or have it a functional DIY project.
